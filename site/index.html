About

I am an experienced software engineer with more than 18 years of experience in web development. I work mostly with Java, Javascript and related technologies but the last couple of years i have also worked with iOS/Objective-C.

Because of my broad knowledge and interests, and because i like to get the complete system working, on most of the projects i work on i am involved in all the parts of the system, from frontend and backend development to deployment and hosting.

I am a quick learner and love to work in a team, especially in agile/SCRUM teams. I love seeing everyone on a team getting better by helping each other.
